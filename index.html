About this object

About

Design made obvious.

OBV studio is a Seoul-based design practice making objects from a single principle: remove everything that isn't essential. We work with stainless steel, cold-roll processes, and small production runs. Every object is made by hand, in limited numbers, and designed to last.

Process

One material.
One process.
One form.

We start with 316L stainless steel sheet and a precise bend radius. Cold-bent, not welded. The form emerges from the material's own logic — not from decoration or styling.
